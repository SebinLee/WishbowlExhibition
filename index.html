Abstract

Increasing participant engagement in a virtual concert space has been actively addressed by numerous researchers. However, despite the various attempts to enhance the virtual concert experience, it is still difficult to catch up with the offline concert experience. To overcome the current limitation of the virtual concert experience, we present the Wishbowl, an immersive virtual concert experience that can enhance participant engagement. Wishbowl provides a virtual concert hall that has been built with triple projectors. Participants can freely join without wearing any devices and move inside the virtual concert hall by rotating their cheering light sticks. Also, at the highlight part of the song, participants can cheer on the virtual artist with virtual light sticks by waving their physical light sticks. To demonstrate the system, we built a prototype content using the virtual idol Verse'day's song named ‘Wishbowl.'
